Taco Cabana

February 7, 2019

An Institution

In a world of fast food representing poor imitations of food that could be, there stands a behemoth whom, atleast in the realm of bean and chesse tacos, not only produces food in a masterfully quick manner, but also captures the truest essence of what that food hopes to achieve. This is the true wonder of Taco Cabana. That a business, a corporate chain, could so effortlessly recreate such paramount tex-mex cuisine at countless locations with staggering consistency.

Cheaper by the Dozen

You might think that this incredible quality and speed would only be accessible to the most upper-class among us, but nay! Each taco you purchase comes at a marginal cost of just above a dollar, potentially cheapened even further when purchased in bulk!
